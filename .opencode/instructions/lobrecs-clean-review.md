# Lobrecs Clean Review

- Lead with bugs, regressions, security issues, and missing verification.
- Ground findings in file and line references.
- Avoid style-only findings unless they hide a real maintainability risk.
- Call out Electron boundary violations and secret persistence immediately.
- Mention residual test gaps even when no blocking findings are found.
